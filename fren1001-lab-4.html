La Vie à l'Université

📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will show the audience around the UNG campus, share your class schedule, and talk about sports and hobbies offered at the university.

Create a 2-minute video showing us around the UNG campus. Share your class schedule (days, times, buildings) and ask 2 questions about the audience's campus.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary table and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 To submit, click Lab #4 above these instructions, or find it in your D2L Assignment Folder.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible
  • What classes does UNG offer? What sports?
  • Describe at least 3 campus locations
  • Days, times, and buildings for your classes
  • Use time expressions: à neuf heures du matin
Ask two questions about the audience's campus or schedule.
Classroom vocabulary, school subjects, days, and time expressions from class.

Telling Time — l'Heure
How to ask and say what time it is
Asking & Telling Time
Quelle heure est-il ?What time is it?
Il est une heure.It is 1:00
Il est deux heures.It is 2:00
Il est midi.Noon
Il est minuit.Midnight
Minutes & Modifiers
et quart:15 — quarter past
et demie:30 — half past
moins le quart:45 — quarter to
du matinAM (morning)
du soirPM (evening)
💡 Il est trois heures et demie du matin. = 3:30 AM — J'ai un cours à neuf heures du matin. = I have class at 9 AM.
