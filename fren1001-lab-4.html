La Vie à l'Université

Click to enlarge
📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will show the audience around the UNG campus, share your class schedule, talk about sports and hobbies, and ask questions about campus life.

Create a 2-minute video showing us around the UNG campus. Share your class schedule (days, times, buildings), mention campus sports, and ask 2 questions about the audience's campus.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary cards and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 Click Lab 4 in your D2L Assignment Folder to submit.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible
  • What classes does UNG offer? What sports?
  • Show or describe at least 3 campus locations
  • Tell us your schedule: days, times, and buildings for your classes
  • Use days of the week and time expressions (à neuf heures du matin)
Ask two questions about the audience's campus or schedule.
Use classroom vocabulary, school subjects, days, and time expressions from class.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed

Telling Time — l'Heure
How to say what time it is in French
Asking & Telling Time
Quelle heure est-il ?What time is it?
Il est une heure.It is 1:00.
Il est deux heures.It is 2:00.
Il est midi.It is noon.
Il est minuit.It is midnight.
Minutes & Modifiers
et quartquarter past (:15)
et demiehalf past (:30)
moins le quartquarter to (:45)
du matinin the morning (AM)
du soirin the evening (PM)
💡 Example: Il est trois heures et demie du matin. = It is 3:30 AM. — J'ai un cours de français à neuf heures du matin. = I have French class at 9 AM.
