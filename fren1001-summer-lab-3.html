📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will give a video tour of your room or house, describe furniture using adjectives and colors, and talk about household chores.

Record a 2-minute video showing us around your dorm or bedroom. Describe furniture, mention 3–4 chores, and ask 2 questions about your audience's home.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary table and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 To submit, click Lab #3 above these instructions, or find it in your D2L Assignment Folder.

  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible; show us your room
  • Describe furniture using adjectives, colors, and numbers
  • Do NOT start every sentence with Il y a…
  • Use prepositions of place (sur, sous, à côté de…)
  • Talk about 3–4 chores you do at home
  • Which is your favorite? Which do you hate?
Ask two questions about the audience's home.
Room/furniture vocabulary and chore verbs from class.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed
