📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will show the audience around the UNG campus, share your class schedule, and talk about sports and hobbies offered at the university.

Create a 2-minute video showing us around the UNG campus. Share your class schedule (days, times, buildings) and mention campus sports.

📍 Online students: You do not need to be physically on campus! Use the UNG website, Google Maps, or campus photos online to describe locations. The goal is to practice French vocabulary — your virtual tour counts.

Telling Time — l’Heure
How to ask and say what time it is
Asking & Telling Time
Quelle heure est-il ?What time is it?[kel ur ay-teel]
Il est une heure.It is 1:00[eel ay oon ur]
Il est midi.Noon[eel ay mee-dee]
Il est minuit.Midnight[eel ay mee-nwee]
Minutes & Modifiers
et quart:15 — quarter past[ay kar]
et demie:30 — half past[ay duh-mee]
de l'après-midiPM (afternoon)[duh lah-pray mee-dee]
du soirPM (evening)[doo swahr]
du matinAM (morning)[doo mah-tan]
moins le quart:45 — quarter to[mwan luh kar]
💡 Il est trois heures et demie du matin. = 3:30 AM
