Key Points to Remember

  • 🕐No late submissions. Folders close at 11:59 PM on Wednesdays. Contact the lab before the deadline if you have technology issues.
  • 📅Don't wait until the last minute. Review each lab's vocabulary and grammar early — practice out loud many times before recording.
  • 📖Only approved resources. Class notes, your textbook, and WordReference.com. Do not read from a prepared script during recording.
  • 🚫Academic misconduct. Using online translators, AI, or outside help may result in serious consequences.
  • 📬Check your email. If due dates or instructions change, you will be notified. Check your UNG email regularly.

Grading

Lab assignments are graded by Language Lab staff — not your course instructor. Grading typically takes about one week. Questions about grades should go directly to the Language Lab.
