Un Voyage à Dahlonega

📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will take the audience on a tour of Dahlonega, give directions in French, talk about your favorite season and the weather, and use the futur proche.

Create a 2-minute video. Tour Dahlonega — choose 4–5 stops from the map and give directions in French. Share your favorite season and describe the weather. Ask 2 questions.

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible
  • Choose 4–5 stops from the Dahlonega map
  • Give directions in French between each stop (tournez à gauche, allez tout droit…)
  • Share your favorite season and explain why
  • Describe the weather during that season
  • Use futur proche: say 3 things you're going to do in that season
Ask two questions about travel or weather preferences.
Use travel, weather, directions vocabulary, and the futur proche from class.

Le Futur Proche
Talking about near-future plans: aller + infinitive
Aller — to go (present)
Jevais
Tuvas
Il / Elle / Onva
Nousallons
Vousallez
Ils / Ellesvont
Examples — Futur Proche
Je vais + infinitifI am going to…
Je vais mangerI'm going to eat
Nous allons voyagerWe're going to travel
Je ne vais pas…I'm not going to…
Il va visiter…He's going to visit…
💡 Formula: Subject + aller (conjugated) + infinitive = near future.
Examples: Je vais jouer au foot samedi. — Nous allons passer du temps avec ma famille. — Dimanche, je ne vais pas travailler.
