📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will take the audience on a tour of Dahlonega, give directions in French, talk about your favorite season and the weather, and use the futur proche.

Create a 2-minute video. Tour Dahlonega with 4–5 stops and give directions in French. Share your favorite season, describe the weather, and use the futur proche.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary table and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 To submit, click Lab #5 above these instructions, or find it in your D2L Assignment Folder.
