📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will take the audience on a tour of Dahlonega, give directions in French, talk about your favorite season and the weather, and use the futur proche.

Create a 2-minute video. Tour Dahlonega with 4–5 stops and give directions in French. Share your favorite season, describe the weather, and use the futur proche.

Le Futur Proche
Conjugated aller + infinitive = near-future plan
Aller — to go
Jevais[zhuh vay]
Tuvas[too vah]
Il/Elle/Onva[eel/el/on vah]
Nousallons[noo zah-lon]
Vousallez[voo zah-lay]
Ils/Ellesvont[eel/el von]
Examples
Je vais mangerI’m going to eat
Nous allons voyagerWe’re going to travel
Je ne vais pas…I’m not going to…
Il va visiter…He’s going to visit…
💡 Je vais jouer au foot samedi. — Je ne vais pas travailler dimanche.
