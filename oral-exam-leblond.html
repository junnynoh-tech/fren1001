Welcome to your final oral exam for FREN 1001. Below you will find 20 question videos recorded by your professor. For each question, your professor will read it twice — listen carefully all the way through, because recording begins automatically as soon as the video ends. Respond in complete French sentences. No notes, no translators, no AI tools.
🚦 How It Works
1
Watch your professor's question video. The question is read twice. Listen all the way through without interruption — focus entirely on understanding the question and mentally preparing your answer.
2
Recording starts automatically the moment the question video ends — there is no pause. You have between 15 and 90 seconds. Speak clearly and in full French sentences from the very first second.
3
Repeat for all 20 questions. Submit your assignment once all responses are complete.
⚠️
Important: Because recording begins the moment the question video ends, there is no buffer between listening and speaking. While listening, use that time to mentally plan your answer — do not wait until the video is over to start thinking.
✅ What Makes a Great Answer ▼
✔ Do this
  • Answer in complete French sentences — never with a simple oui or non alone.
  • Expand on your answer. Add a detail, an example, or a follow-up thought.
  • Speak naturally and at a steady pace — imagine a conversation with your professor.
  • Draw on vocabulary and grammar from Chapitres 1–6 studied throughout the semester.
✘ Avoid this
  • Answering with just oui or non — it will not earn full credit.
  • Reading from a script, notes, or any written material. Strictly prohibited — your screen is shared and any suspicion results in a zero.
  • Using online translators or AI tools to prepare answers.
  • Stopping after the bare minimum when more is clearly expected.
💡 Example — Topic : Le ménage
Question : Est-ce que tu fais le ménage chez toi ?
✗ Insufficient — Oui, je fais le ménage chez moi.
Grammatically fine, but far too short for full credit.
✓ Full credit — Oui, je fais le ménage chez moi. Je fais la lessive et je lave le sol. Mon frère fait la vaisselle et il enlève la poussière.
This is exactly the kind of answer that earns full credit.
