📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will practice introducing yourself, sharing about your life as a university student, and asking basic questions — all in French!

Record a 1.5–2 minute video introducing yourself: name, age, nationality, personality, and reasons for studying French. Ask 2 questions to your audience.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary table and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 To submit, click Lab #1 above these instructions, or find it in your D2L Assignment Folder.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format
Click to enlargePhoto: Vitaly Gariev · Unsplash
📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 1.5–2 minutes long
  • Face visible throughout
  • Record in a quiet location
  • A greeting
  • Your name: Je m'appelle…
  • Your age: J'ai __ ans.
  • Nationality / where you're from
  • Why you study French (1–2 reasons)
  • At least 3 adjectives for your personality
  • Use Je suis… + adjectives
  • Brief physical description
End your video by asking two questions to your audience in French.
Use vocabulary and expressions from Unit 1 covered in class.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed
