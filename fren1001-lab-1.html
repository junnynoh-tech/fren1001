📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will practice introducing yourself, sharing about your life as a university student, and asking basic questions — all in French!

Record yourself introducing who you are: your name, age, nationality, personality, and why you study French. Ask two questions to your audience.

🚦Process
  1. 1Click Vocabulary below to study words and hear pronunciations.
  2. 2Open Grammar if you need a conjugation reference.
  3. 3Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  4. 4Click Lab 1: Video Assignment above — Bongo opens to record & submit.

🔗 Click Lab 1: Video Assignment at the top of this page to open Bongo — record and submit your video directly there.

📦Materials
  • 🔗 Submitted via Bongo
  • 📹 Face must be visible
Click to enlargeVitaly Gariev · Unsplash
✅Requirements
  • 1.5–2 minutes long
  • Face visible throughout
  • Quiet location
  • Greeting
  • Name: Je m’appelle…
  • Age: J’ai __ ans.
  • Nationality / where you’re from
  • Why you study French
  • At least 3 personality adjectives using Je suis…
  • Brief physical description
End by asking two questions to your audience in French.
Use Chapitre 1 vocabulary from class.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed
