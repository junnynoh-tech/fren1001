Tout sur moi

📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will practice introducing yourself, sharing about your life as a university student, and asking basic questions — all in French!

Record yourself introducing who you are: your name, age, nationality, personality, and why you study French. Ask two questions to your audience at the end.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary cards and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 Click Lab 1 in your D2L Assignment Folder to submit.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format
Click to enlarge
📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 1.5–2 minutes long
  • Face visible in the video
  • Record in a quiet location
  • A greeting (e.g., Bonjour !)
  • Your name: Je m'appelle...
  • Your age: J'ai __ ans.
  • Your nationality / where you're from
  • Why you study French (1–2 reasons)
  • At least 3 adjectives describing your personality
  • Use Je suis + adjectives
  • Brief physical description
End your video by asking two questions to your audience in French. Example: Et vous, comment vous appelez-vous ?
Use the vocabulary and expressions from Unit 1 covered in class. Incorporate varied adjectives and verb forms.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed
