🎙️ Oral Final Exam — Instructions

Your oral final exam is a live, spoken assessment conducted by your course instructor. Each professor has their own format, topics, and scheduling process — select your instructor below to view their specific instructions and sign-up information.

  • 🗣️Exams are conducted in French. Prepare to speak spontaneously — no reading from notes.
  • 📅Scheduling is handled by your instructor. Check their page for available time slots.
  • 📋Review your instructor's rubric and topic list carefully before your appointment.
  • 🚫No translators, AI tools, or written scripts during the exam.
