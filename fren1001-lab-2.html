Ma Famille

Click to enlargePhoto: Jimmy Dean · Unsplash
📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will talk about your family, review family vocabulary and -ER verbs, and describe family members using adjectives and ages.

Record a 2-minute video introducing your family. Show a family photo or draw a family tree. Describe at least 3 family members and yourself.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary table and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 To submit, click Lab #2 above these instructions, or find it in your D2L Assignment Folder.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible; include a photo or family tree drawing
  • Brief self-introduction (greeting, name, age)
  • Describe at least 3 family members: hair, eyes, 3 adjectives
  • Include ages: Mon père a 50 ans.
Ask two questions to your audience about their family.
