La Maison

📝Summary & Context

In this lab you will give a video tour of your room or house, describe the furniture using adjectives and colors, and talk about household chores.

Record a 2-minute video showing us around your dorm or bedroom. Describe furniture with adjectives and colors, mention 3–4 chores, and ask 2 questions about your audience's home.

🚦Process
  1. 1Study the vocabulary cards and grammar below.
  2. 2Practice speaking out loud — many times!
  3. 3Record via Zoom or phone (MP4 or MOV).
  4. 4Upload to your D2L Assignment Folder.

📂 Click Lab 3 in your D2L Assignment Folder to submit.

📦What You Will Need
  • 🎥 Zoom or phone/computer
  • 🎞️ MP4 or MOV format

📖Grammar Review
✅Requirements
  • 2 minutes long
  • Face visible; show us the room
  • Describe the furniture using adjectives, colors, and numbers
  • Do NOT start every sentence with Il y a… — vary your expressions
  • Use prepositions of place (sur, sous, à côté de…)
  • Tell us about 3–4 chores you do at home
  • Which chore is your favorite? Which do you hate?
Ask two questions about the audience's home. Example: Qu'y a-t-il dans votre chambre ?
Incorporate room/furniture vocabulary and chore verbs from class.
🚫 No online translator or AI allowed

Prepositions of Place
Use these to describe where objects are located in your home
suron (top of)
sousunder
dansin / inside
devantin front of
derrièrebehind
à côté denext to
entrebetween
en face deacross from / facing
près denear
loin defar from
💡 Examples: Le lit est près de la fenêtre. (The bed is near the window.) — Le bureau est devant le miroir. (The desk is in front of the mirror.) — Le canapé est entre les deux chaises.
